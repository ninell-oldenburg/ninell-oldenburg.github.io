I'm primarily interested in the narratives around AI and what we can learn from it about ourselves. This includes questions like:

+ Why are there so dratsically different views on the risks of AI?
+ How are the near-term and long-term visions of AI constructed, i.e., why should or shouldn't it have consciousness?
+ What is the actual and narrated use of the term "intelligence"?

I like to look at these things from different angles and in different granularity. This includes academic research, journalism, blogging, and art.
